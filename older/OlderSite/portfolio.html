Crawford the Cat

 

Crawford the Cat teaches 100 Sight Words and important life lessons.

 

Sight Words are commonly used words that early readers are required to master. In this app, Crawford the Cat teaches 100 Sight Words in a fun and interactive way. Kids interact with ABC blocks to spell the words. Every time your child spells 10 words correctly, they are rewarded with a new "Video Block". Video Blocks are special blocks that can used to play one of the 13 "How-To" videos.

 

Crawford's "How To" videos in this app will help teach important life lessons. The 13 short videos included in this app will teach things like; Hand Washing, Exercise, Cooperation, Manners, Responsibility, Teeth Brushing, Nutrition, and MUCH MORE!

 

Technology:

iOS SDK 7, Unity 4.0, Core Animation, iPad app

 

App Store link:   https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/crawfords-sight-words-video/id905010775?mt=8
